How It Works

Voice Control

Athena recognises voice commands and executes your request. You can also type when you do not want to speak.

Auto Sync Across Devices

Have multiple devices? Athena automatically syncs your data across all devices. All you have to do is give it such permission.

Set Reminders and Schedule Messages

Not only does Athena notify you about things, it also lets you set reminders on specific activities you would like to be reminded about. You can schedule a message or enable an auto-reply.

Safe & Secure

Athena recognises that the privacy of eveyone is important and does not steal your information or sell it to others. It is trustworthy.
